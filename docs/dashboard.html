Retention & lifecycle

SMB cohort retention

Cumulative % of enrolled SMBs still transacting by month N

Finding

3-month retention trends from 97.6% (Oct 2024 cohort) down to 88.6% (Dec 2025 cohort). Worth investigating whether channel-mix drift or onboarding capacity is pulling down recent-cohort stickiness as enrollments scale 5×.

Virtual card first-time success

Monthly rate by card network, against the 78% target

Finding

Rate stabilizes near target once monthly volume clears ~4,000 VCs (Feb 2025). Early-period dips are sampling noise from low volume and should not drive decisions. Baseline metric for the Director of Fraud Strategy hire.

Acquisition & operations

Channel acquisition efficiency

Activation rate vs. blended CAC; bubble area = wasted CAC

Finding

Direct sales wastes ~$138K on never-activated SMBs — 80× the waste of organic, despite comparable activation rates. Primary candidate for channel-mix rebalance under the 65% COGS reduction mandate.

Reconciliation exception queue

Captured invoices requiring Ops review, by severity and SMB size

Finding

9,910 total exceptions on 97,769 captured invoices. Large SMBs generate the majority of dollar-weighted risk despite being only 10% of the base, supporting a tiered SLA for ops prioritization.

Data quality

Planted bugs caught before reaching marts

Two realistic data-quality bugs were intentionally planted in the generator and caught by the pytest suite before any mart rebuilt on top of the corrupted raw data.

Decimal-scale bug

499 / 98,644 captures (0.51%) had interchange_fee written in basis points instead of dollars (e.g. $134,455 on a $50K capture). Caught by ratio test fee / amount < 10%, fixed with a transactional SQL cleanup.

Orphan VC bug

197 / 125,974 virtual cards (0.16%) had authorization events but no vc_issued event upstream, corrupting time-to-capture timing. Caught by existence assertion; cleanup synthesized missing issuance rows tagged evt_fix_*.
